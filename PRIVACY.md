# Privacy Policy for microReel

**Effective Date:** March 17, 2026

microReel is committed to providing a premium, non-intrusive video overlay experience while respecting user privacy. This policy outlines our "Local-First" approach to data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
microReel operates primarily on your local device. We disclose the following data interactions:

*   **Website Content:** microReel reads text content from supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and GitHub Copilot) to detect when a response is being generated and to trigger the appropriate video overlay.
*   **User Activity:** The extension monitors user interactions (such as clicks or unmuting) within the overlay to provide a seamless playback experience.
*   **Location:** Incidental to fetching video metadata (via YouTube oEmbed) and the curated `videos.json` manifest from GitHub, your IP address may be visible to those third-party services. microReel does not store or track your location.

## 2. Data Storage
*   **No Remote Storage:** We do not send your prompts, AI responses, or browsing history to any external servers.
*   **Local Settings:** Your preferences (theme, positioning, enabled sites) are stored locally using `chrome.storage.sync`.

## 3. Third-Party Services
microReel embeds content via YouTube iFrames. Your interaction with these iFrames is subject to [YouTube's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. Disclosures
*   We do **not** sell or transfer user data to third parties.
*   We do **not** use user data for purposes unrelated to the extension's single purpose (providing a video overlay during AI generation).
*   We do **not** use user data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

## 5. Contact
For questions regarding this policy, please open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
